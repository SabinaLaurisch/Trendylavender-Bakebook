Lavender Cheesecake Recipe

A delicious and elegant cheesecake infused with the subtle flavor of lavender.

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 cups graham cracker crumbs
  • 1/2 cup butter, melted
  • 3 packages cream cheese, softened
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 3 eggs
  • 2 tbsp culinary lavender

    • Instructions:

  • Preheat oven to 325°F (160°C).
  • Mix graham cracker crumbs and melted butter; press into the bottom of a 9-inch springform pan.
  • Beat cream cheese, sugar, and vanilla with an electric mixer until smooth.
  • Add eggs one at a time, beating after each addition. Stir in lavender.
  • Pour mixture over crust. Bake for 55-60 minutes or until center is set.
  • Cool, then refrigerate for at least 4 hours before serving.
